Titan Gradnja

The history that led to the creation of TITAN GRADNJA begins more than 30 years ago, with the family company TRAKSIM, whose activity was the production and processing of plastics. Over time, company’s activity has changed and then it became a construction company. That is when the Building concept, the parent company and the company whose activities were the production of PVC joinery, construction of residential and commercial buildings, investment, etc. was created. BuildingConcept d.o.o. based in Belgrade, has been operating successfully in the field of construction for more than a decade. BuildingConcept was founded as a trading company for the sale of construction materials, in the course of its business it expanded its activities to the production of PVC facade joinery and the construction of high-rise buildings. Thanks to the professional attitude towards work, qualification structure and internal organization, which includes a minimum of administrative and non-productive staff, we have achieved rapid development of the company. Baja building and Building concept are companies that contributed to the creation of TITAN GRADJE, which also united the business of these companies. As TITAN GRADNJA, we appeared on the market in 2013, and since then we have been running projects such as: Pašmanska (Lekino brdo), Smiljanićeva (upgrade), Ruža vetrova-Mirijevo
