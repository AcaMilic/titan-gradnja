Titan Gradnja

The history that led to the creation of TITAN GRADNJA begins more than 30 years ago, with the family company TRAKSIM, whose activity was the production and processing of plastics. Over time, company’s activity has changed and then it became a construction company. That is when the Building concept, the parent company and the company whose activities were the production of PVC joinery, construction of residential and commercial buildings, investment, etc. was created. BuildingConcept d.o.o. based in Belgrade, has been operating successfully in the field of construction for more than a decade.
